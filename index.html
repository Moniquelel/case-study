Melomaniac Mobile App Concept Design

THE PROJECT:
For this project we developed a mobile application centered around helping young adults find shows for local artists in their area. I had a hand in the entire design process from concept to iteration.
MY RESPONSIBILITIES:
User Testing Competitive analysis Empathy map Wireframes Low/HIgh Fidelity Prototypes

RESEARCH

Build Research Plan In order to gather more information, we first sent out a short survey via Google that asks general questions about travel to gauge general travel patterns and feeling. Conduct One on One Interviews and Send out Survey Then, we conducted six 15-20 minute interviews to understand the userâ€™s needs and pain points with finding local concerts. Review Data and Asses After reviewing the qualitative and quantitative data we will compose a user persona. Objectives: To understand how young adults find and attend concerts in their local community, and any roadblocks they run into along the way. Do people struggle with finding local music? Is there a way we can make finding local music easier?

AFFINITY DIAGRAM:

We conducted multiple one on one user interviews and synthesized the findings into an affinity diagram. We did this to better understand the gap in the market, and understand any current gaps with existing applications. Additionally we wanted to understand the core needs of users and the most efficient way to solve them.


I LIKE, I WISH , WHAT IF:

As a team we participated in the I Like, I Wish, What If practice. During this step we took a deep look at what we liked about current music applications on the Market. What features we desired in those apps and then imagined any scenario that could not only fill any gaps but create solutions to unidentified problems.

USER PERSONA:

To better understand our user we combined thew interviews to create echo stark. Echo symbolizes a typical young adult who would utilize our application. We needed to understand her goals and motivations to better understand how our user would make decisions.

USER SCENARIO:

To dig into a users frustrations and roadblocks while trying to find a ticket to a concert we theorised a typical user journey. Going through each step in the process and analyzing typical thoughts and feelings a user would go through. This helped us understand a simple and clear flow a user may take while using our application.

WIREFRAMES:

Before creating any visual designs or prototypes we created wireframes. This ensured we had a clear user path for our application. As well it allowed us to do guerilla testing and usability testing to confirm we had a clear path for the user to take to accomplish their task. It also allowed us the time to create clear hierarchy and readability before we implemented visual design.

PROTOTYPE:

For our final prototype we implemented a consistent design. We wanted an inviting color scheme as well as bold animations. Additionally we wanted to make sure there was strong accessibility with our type, colors, and sizing. Guaranteeing any user would easily be able to use our application.
